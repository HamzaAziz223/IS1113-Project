My Interest in Boxing

My Interest in Boxing began when I was watching a fight with my father on television. I was amazed at the athletes fitness and their level of endurance. How they could take the hits and keep moving forward. I never fancied myself as a great boxer as I am asthmatic but i feel i can do ok in the ring now after a semester boxing in the mardyke. My favourite current boxer is Anthony Joshua as he is an impressive role model. His boxing identity is based and respect and integrity. I feel boxing is a great sport to get into as it teaches people humility, respect, and endurance. I would highly recommend anyone to try it. Its very ideal for me as I am also interested in Weightlifting and the gym so it a perfect combination. Involving both muscle building and fat burning through intense workouts and cardio.
