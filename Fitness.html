The Gym

My Story with the gym is about as typical as you could guess. A skinny kid who always envied everyone else who looked normal. So i was naturally attracted to the gym. I remember i used to watch videos of intense workouts and try to replicate them myself at a young age. But i was always told it is dangerous to start weightlifting until the age of 15 as it can effect a persons height. So i patiently waited until i was 15 and then i started. It was very basic at the start, i just had a few dumbbells at home, numerous online workout plans and determination. But i knew this would not be enough i actually needed to go to the gym. So in transition year i joined my school gym. Progress was slow but with the help of my friends and research i managed to finally make progress. I fell in love with the whole aspect instantly. The regiment, the eating, the commitment, and of course the results. It has changed my life for the better and I feel more confident as a result. I would once again highly recommend it to anyone who is considering joining as it is never too late. I drew inspiration from classics like Arnold, and modern bodybuilders such as Calum Von Moger. I find it very interesting reading about their mindsets, their ideologies etc.
