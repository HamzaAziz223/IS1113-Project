Work Experience

In transition year when i was studying in Rockwell College, I had a manditory 2 week work experience. During this time i spent a week in a solicitors office; John Shee Solicitors Clonmel, and a week in South Tipperary General Hospital in Clonmel. While working in the Solicitors Office, i worked mostly as a secretary as well as a solicitor to some degree. I had to answer calls, photocopy and print documents, Research cases, deliver letters etc. I gained confidence while speaking to clients, and learned to listen carefully to their wants and take note. I watched how professional solicitors dealt with clients, their manner of speaking, body movements, language etc. In the Hospital for the first few days we were assigned to a team of doctors who did rounds of the numerous wards. It was quite similar to the solicitors office in the sense that i learned about their manner of speaking, body movements, language etc while speaking to patients. I also spent a few days in the surgery ward and we were shown around the hospital. We also attended a few presentations that doctors have to give on a regular basis.
